About project

Using this app you can browse the list of registered restaurants, place orders in their menu and manage your orders.

You can sign up as a client or as an owner of restaurants. If you sign up as the owner then all the client's functionality is also available to you.

If you sign up as the owner then you can register your restaurants, manage them and their client's orders.

To save your time you can try this app using an existing account of the owner. Login and password: aaa.

Please, don't delete the restaurant if use.
