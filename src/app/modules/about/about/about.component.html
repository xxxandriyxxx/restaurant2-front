About the project

Using this app you can browse the list of registered restaurants, place orders in their menu and manage your orders.

You can sign up as a client or as an owner of restaurants.

If you sign up as the owner:

- you can register your restaurants, manage them and their client's orders.

- all the client's functionality is also available to you.
